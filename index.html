Eligibility

Age : The candidate should be at least 17 years old as on december 31st of the year of admission

Qualifying Examination : The candidate should have passed the 12th standard examination or equivalent from a recognized board with physics, chemistry, and biology (PCB) as compulsory subjects

Minimum Marks : The candidate should have scored a minimum of 50% marks in PCB combined for general category students and 40% marks for reserved category students(ST/SC/OBC)

NEET Qualification : From 2019 onwards, it is mandatory for Indian students to qualify for the National Eligibility cum Entrence Test(NEET) to pursure MBBS abroad.
